Tyler Kilbourne has a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Information Technology from Arizona State University (ASU) with a focus area in web development. He currently works for ASU as Web Manager at the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences. He has been developing for the web since 2006 and his interests include open source content management systems (such as Drupal and Moodle), user centered design, and educational gaming. He has experice building websites for marketing small and mediums sized businesses, web applications with rich functionality and a variety of Drupal based web projects. Drupal experience includes extensive custom module and theme development and work on large enterprise Drupal sites. His hobbies include photography and developing web based computer games.
